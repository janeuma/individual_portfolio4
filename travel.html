Barcelona

This past May, I went to Barcelona on a three-week study abroad program. While there, I took a class on design thinking. The main goal of the class was to learn the design thinking path with an emphasis on user empathy while working in teams. We would pick a product to reinvent, interview people, and use those user responses to make a better product. In addition, Barcelona is such a solid place to study abroad there is such a good city, mountains, beaches, there is also so much to do. Every day, we ended class at around 1:30 and had the rest of the day to explore. If we did not feel like touring that day, we would go to the beach or lay in a park. The attractions there are so beautiful like La Sagrada Familia, La Parc Guell, and the bunkers. The food is also very good, specifically the paella. It is such an amazing place to spend three weeks studying and exploring.
